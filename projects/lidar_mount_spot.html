LiDAR Mount for Boston Dynamics Spot

Lightweight 3D‑printed truss tower mount with an acrylic top plate, ensuring 7–9″ arm clearance and stability under motion.

Structures3D PrintingRobotics

Problem

Mount a LiDAR above the stowed arm while maintaining clearance, minimizing weight, and mitigating vibration.

Approach

  • Designed a 5×5×8 in truss tower; analyzed stiffness-to-mass tradeoffs.
  • Added damping features and a modular top plate for sensor swaps.
  • Validated fit and stability through motion tests and imagery.

Outcome

Stable during gait transitions; easy install to cover with six-hole pattern; improved sensor FOV.
