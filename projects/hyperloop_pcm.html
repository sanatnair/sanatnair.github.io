Hyperloop PCM Cooling System

Closed-loop subsystem with central PCM heat exchanger keeping the coolant near room temperature before entering the cooling plates.

ThermalDFM/DFACADTesting

Problem

Maintain motor thermal limits under propulsion duty cycles with minimal mass and volume, enabling reliable performance during runs.

Approach

  • Modeled heat load and transient response; sized PCM mass and heat exchanger surface area.
  • Designed plumbing to return coolant to near room temperature before plate entry.
  • Prototyped and validated flow using pressure drop estimates and pump curve selection.

Outcome

Achieved target ΔT across plates with acceptable pressure drop; modular design eases maintenance and integration.
